DAPP II
===========================


**Duration : 05APR - 23APR**

**Instructor : Dave Mckay**

Course Description
------------------

The focus of this course will be on the research, ideation, development, and presentation process. Students will work on a showcase project reflecting the culmination of skills acquired throughout the year. They will be challenged to select a business use case, apply project management principles and best practices, create a pitch, and develop a presentation-ready decentralised application. Students will actively explore the differences between a business presentation, an investment presentation, and a technical presentation in a simulated professional environment.
